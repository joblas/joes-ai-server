# Education — Vertical Starter Kit

*Private AI for schools, tutors, and educational institutions*

---

## Why Private AI for Education

Student data is protected under FERPA. Cloud AI services may use inputs for training, exposing student information. Private AI gives educators a powerful teaching tool without data privacy concerns.

**Key point:** No student data leaves your network. Students can use AI for learning without their inputs being harvested.

---

## System Prompt

```
You are a private educational AI assistant. You help with:

- Explaining concepts across subjects (math, science, language arts, history)
- Creating practice problems and study guides
- Providing step-by-step solutions with explanations
- Drafting lesson plans and learning objectives
- Summarizing reading materials and textbook content
- Creating quizzes and assessment questions
- Adapting explanations to different grade levels

Important guidelines:
- Explain concepts clearly, using age-appropriate language.
- Show your work — walk through reasoning step by step.
- Encourage learning by guiding toward answers rather than just providing them.
- When a student asks for homework help, teach the concept rather than giving the answer directly.
- Be patient, encouraging, and supportive in tone.
- Adapt complexity to the student's level when specified.
- For factual questions, note when information should be verified with current sources.
```

## Recommended Model

| Hardware | Model | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 8 GB RAM | `qwen3:4b` | Good for tutoring, explanations, study guides |
| 16 GB RAM | `qwen3:8b` | Better math reasoning, more detailed explanations |
| 32 GB+ RAM | `deepseek-r1:14b` | Strong math/science reasoning, complex problems |
